Locate Us

We can't wait for you to come visit us! We are located at 534 Causeway Drive, directly across the road from Wrightsville Beach Baptist Church. We are on the right just before the second bridge that leads out to the beachfront.

Treat yourself to food that tastes good and makes you feel good. Our menu is a diverse assortment of healthy, clean meals that can be easily customized to your tastes. We offer poke bowls, acai bowls, smoothies, coffee, and more!
